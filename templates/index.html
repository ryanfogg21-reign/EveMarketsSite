Calculation notes
  • Buy ISK / % – Revenue = product sold to best buy order (sales tax only); Cost = materials from cheapest sell orders + manufacturing tax. No broker fee since you're filling an existing order.
  • Opt. ISK / % – Revenue = product posted as sell order (broker fee + sales tax); Cost = materials bought via buy orders (broker fee added) + manufacturing tax.
  • 7d Avg (Buy/Opt) – Same fee structure but using ESI average_price (universe-wide 7-day volume-weighted average) for both product and materials.
  • Manufacturing Tax = Σ(material adjusted_price × effective_qty) × SCI%. Adjusted price comes from CCP's ESI market prices endpoint.
  • ME% – Each 1% reduces material quantities: eff_qty = max(1, ⌈base_qty × (1 − ME/100)⌉)
  • IpH – Profit ÷ base manufacturing time (seconds ÷ 3600). Does not apply TE research reduction.
  • Saturation % – Listed sell volume ÷ 7-day average daily traded volume × 100. Sourced from Jita (The Forge, region 10000002).
